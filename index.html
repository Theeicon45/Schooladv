Head teacher's message

Some things we know, some things we don’t. But this we know! Quality education is a right for all children. It’s for this reason that we in the St Bakhita Equator school strive to give the very best. Our nurturing of the young minds is holistic. We pride ourselves in giving our learners the best environment for growing academically, spiritually, and in all other aspects of life. We highly value the students and, most importantly, the parents for allowing their children to be part of our great institution.


Our success story is based on the fundamental pillars of discipline, hard work, spiritual nourishment, teamwork, and a high level of ownership of our learners. If there is a school I would recommend, it is St Bakhita not only because I am the head teacher but also because of the success of the school in its short existence and its high potential. Apart from the normal curriculum, we offer extracurricular activities such as chess, karate, and also offer foreign language classes such as French, German, and Italian. My final remark is bringing your child to St Bakhita Equator school and immerse them in an academically focused, religious, and serene environment for them to grow both academically and religiously.
