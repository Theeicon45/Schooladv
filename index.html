Head teacher message Some things we know, some things we don’t. But this we know! quality education quality education is a right for all Children. It’s for this reason that we in the St Bakhita Equator school strive to give the very best. Our nurturing of the young minds is holistic. We pride ourselves is giving our learners the best environment for growing academically, spiritually and in all other aspects of life. We highly value the students and most importantly the parents for allowing their children to be part of our great institution. Our success story is based on the fundamental pillars of discipline, hard work, spiritual nourishment, team work and high level of owning our learners. If there is a school, I would recommend is St Bakhita not because I am the head teacher but because the success of the school in its short existence and the high potential of the school. Apart from the normal curriculum we offer extra curriculum activities such as chess, karate and also offer foreign language classes such as French, German and Italian. my final remark is bringing your child to St Bakhita Equator school and immerse them in an academically focused, religious and serene environment for them to grow both academically and religiously.
