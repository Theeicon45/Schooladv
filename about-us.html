Our Vision

At St Bakhita Equator, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional services to schools and students, aiming to achieve outstanding results. We believe in making a positive difference in education and society. Our team is focused on providing top-notch support and constantly seeking improvement and creativity.

Our Mission

Our mission at St Bakhita Equator is to empower schools and students by delivering innovative and personalized educational services. Through our unwavering dedication to excellence, we strive to foster a nurturing learning environment where every individual can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. By continually refining our approaches and embracing creativity, we aim to make a meaningful and lasting impact on education and society as a whole.

Comprehensive history

St Bakhita equator school was launched on January 2011 as St Theresa school as an ECDE CENTRE with only two students in preschool. It was headed by Tr Jane Nduru. The founding Director was Rev Fr Boniface Mwangi.
The school has been under the stewardship of several directors including:
  • Rev Fr Ezekiel Thuita
  • Rev Fr Charles Thumbi
  • Rev Fr John Gichuki
  • Director Rev Fr George Kinuthia(The current one)
In the year 2015 under the stewardship of rev Fr Ezekiel Thuita, the school changed from St Teresa to St Bakhita Equator.
St Bakhita Equator was launched and blessed by His Grace Emeritus Most Reverend Archbishop Peter Kairu on 25th January 2015.
The school only offers the CBC curriculum
