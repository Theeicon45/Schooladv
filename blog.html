Achieving Academic Success

Students in the lab engage in collaborative experiments, fostering hands-on learning and scientific exploration. They work together, applying theoretical knowledge to practical situations, enhancing their problem-solving skills and gaining valuable insights into real-world applications of academic concepts.

Unlocking Academic Potential Through Play

Play is more than just a way for children to pass the time—it's a crucial aspect of their development. Through play, children learn valuable social, cognitive, and emotional skills that lay the foundation for future success. Whether it's engaging in imaginative play, exploring the outdoors, or participating in organized sports, playing allows children to develop creativity, problem-solving abilities, and resilience. Moreover, play promotes physical health and well-being, fostering strong muscles, coordination, and a healthy lifestyle. At St Bakhita Equator, we recognize the immense benefits of play and encourage children to explore, experiment, and have fun as they learn and grow.

The Importance of Spiritual Nourishment in Education

At St Bakhita Equator, we recognize that true education goes beyond academics—it encompasses the spiritual growth and well-being of our students. Our partnership with St Teresa Equator Parish , located adjacent to our school grounds, provides a unique opportunity for spiritual nourishment and enrichment. Through regular chapel services, prayer groups, and religious education classes, students have the chance to deepen their faith, explore their spirituality, and develop a strong moral compass. The presence of St Teresa Equator Parish within our school community not only fosters a sense of belonging and unity but also reinforces our commitment to holistic education, where the mind, body, and spirit are nurtured in tandem. Together, we strive to cultivate compassionate, empathetic individuals who are grounded in their faith and equipped to make positive contributions to the world.

Green Energy and Environmental Conservation

At St Bakhita Equator, we believe in empowering students to become stewards of the environment, and one way we do this is through the integration of solar energy education into our curriculum. By teaching students about the benefits of solar energy and its role in mitigating climate change, we inspire them to think critically about sustainability and renewable energy solutions. Our solar energy initiatives not only provide hands-on learning opportunities but also contribute to reducing our carbon footprint and promoting environmental conservation. Through the installation of solar panels on campus buildings, students witness firsthand the power of solar energy in action, powering our classrooms, labs, and facilities with clean, renewable energy. By embracing solar technology, we demonstrate our commitment to creating a more sustainable future for generations to come and instill in our students a sense of responsibility towards the planet they call home.

Sports Training for Skill Development

At St Bakhita Equator, we understand the invaluable role skilled teachers play in shaping well-rounded individuals. That's why we're thrilled to offer specialized sports training programs for our educators. Through these programs, our teachers enhance their knowledge and proficiency in various sports, enabling them to provide top-notch coaching and guidance to our students. By investing in our teachers' skill development, we not only elevate the quality of our sports programs but also inspire our students to excel both on and off the field. With passionate and knowledgeable educators leading the way, we're committed to fostering a culture of athleticism, teamwork, and sportsmanship at St Bakhita Equator school.

Culinary Excellence

At St Bakhita Equator, we believe that nutritious meals are essential for student success. That's why we're proud to partner with skilled and ethical chefs to ensure the success of our school feeding program. Our chefs are not only experts in their craft but also committed to sourcing high-quality, locally sourced ingredients to create wholesome and delicious meals for our students. With a focus on nutrition and sustainability, our chefs design menus that not only satisfy hunger but also nourish young minds and bodies. By prioritizing culinary excellence and ethical practices, we're not just feeding our students; we're empowering them to thrive and reach their full potential. Join us in celebrating the dedication and talent of our culinary team as they continue to make a difference in the lives of our students every day.
Guidance and counselling Department

At St Bakhita Equator, we believe in nurturing not just academic excellence, but also the holistic development of every student. That's why our comprehensive guidance and counseling program is designed to provide personalized support tailored to your child's unique needs. From academic challenges to social-emotional growth and career readiness, our experienced team is here to empower your child to thrive both inside and outside the classroom.


By choosing St Bakhita Equator, you're not just enrolling your child in a school – you're joining a supportive community dedicated to their success. We understand the importance of fostering resilience, self-confidence, and a love for learning, and our guidance and counseling program is just one of the many ways we support your child's journey towards a bright and promising future.

At our school, agriculture isn't just a subject—it's a living, breathing part of our educational ecosystem. Through our hands-on approach to learning, students delve into the world of agriculture, where they tend to our cows, pigs, and maize farm with care and dedication. These experiences go beyond textbooks, allowing students to witness the interconnectedness of nature firsthand. By nurturing animals and crops, students develop vital skills in responsibility, empathy, and environmental stewardship.


Our agriculture program isn't just about what happens on the farm—it's about cultivating a community of learners who are passionate about making a difference. As students engage with the rhythms of nature, they gain a deeper appreciation for the food we eat and the world we inhabit. Through school agriculture, we sow the seeds of knowledge and compassion, nurturing not only future farmers but also future leaders committed to building a sustainable and equitable world for generations to come. Join us as we grow together, one harvest at a time.

At St Bakhita Equator, the safety and well-being of our students are our top priorities, and our school transportation system reflects this commitment. Equipped with modern amenities and operated by experienced and licensed drivers, our fleet of school buses provides a safe and secure mode of transportation for students to and from school. With regular maintenance checks and adherence to strict safety protocols, parents can have peace of mind knowing that their children are in good hands during their daily commute.


In addition to safety, we also prioritize convenience in our school transportation system. With designated bus stops located conveniently throughout the community and efficient routing, we strive to minimize travel time and ensure that students arrive at school promptly and ready to learn. Our friendly and professional bus drivers create a welcoming atmosphere on board, making the journey to school an enjoyable and stress-free experience for students of all ages.
