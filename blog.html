Guidance and counselling Department

At St Bakhita Equator, we believe in nurturing not just academic excellence, but also the holistic development of every student. That's why our comprehensive guidance and counseling program is designed to provide personalized support tailored to your child's unique needs. From academic challenges to social-emotional growth and career readiness, our experienced team is here to empower your child to thrive both inside and outside the classroom.


By choosing St Bakhita Equator, you're not just enrolling your child in a school – you're joining a supportive community dedicated to their success. We understand the importance of fostering resilience, self-confidence, and a love for learning, and our guidance and counseling program is just one of the many ways we support your child's journey towards a bright and promising future.

At our school, agriculture isn't just a subject—it's a living, breathing part of our educational ecosystem. Through our hands-on approach to learning, students delve into the world of agriculture, where they tend to our cows, pigs, and maize farm with care and dedication. These experiences go beyond textbooks, allowing students to witness the interconnectedness of nature firsthand. By nurturing animals and crops, students develop vital skills in responsibility, empathy, and environmental stewardship.


Our agriculture program isn't just about what happens on the farm—it's about cultivating a community of learners who are passionate about making a difference. As students engage with the rhythms of nature, they gain a deeper appreciation for the food we eat and the world we inhabit. Through school agriculture, we sow the seeds of knowledge and compassion, nurturing not only future farmers but also future leaders committed to building a sustainable and equitable world for generations to come. Join us as we grow together, one harvest at a time.
