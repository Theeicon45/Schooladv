Comprehensive history

St Bakhita equator school was launched on January 2011 as St Theresa school as an ECDE CENTRE with only two students in preschool. It was headed by Tr Jane Nduru. The founding Director was Rev Fr Boniface Mwangi.
The school has been under the stewardship of several directors including:
  • Rev Fr Ezekiel Thuita
  • Rev Fr Charles Thumbi
  • Rev Fr John Gichuki
  • Director Rev Fr George Kinuthia(The current one)
In the year 2015 under the stewardship of rev Fr Ezekiel Thuita, the school changed from St Teresa to St Bakhita Equator.
St Bakhita Equator was launched and blessed by His Grace Emeritus Most Reverend Archbishop Peter Kairu on 25th January 2015.
The school only offers the CBC curriculum
